Additional Controls
===================

CELESTE
-------
E - Toggle screen shake

CELESTE Practice Mod (v600m)
----------------------------
S - Previous level
F - Next level
E - Toggle HUD
D - Toggle BG Music
A - Toggle gemskip (2300m+)
P - Pause menu
    > "Reset SOB" - Clears saved best times
    > "Seed RNG" - Toggles RNG seeding
        > "Seed++" - Increment RNG seed
        > "Seed--" - Decrement RNG seed
